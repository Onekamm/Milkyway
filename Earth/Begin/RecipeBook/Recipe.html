Ackee and Saltfish Recipe!!

This is a recipe that shows some love to a childhood favourite of mine :)) Pulled straight from the caribbean boasting authentic flavours and vibrant colour.

Ingredients

  • Ackee
  • Saltfish
  • Scotch Bonnet
  • Assorted Peppers
  • 3 Onions

Instructions

  1. Sear Onions
  2. Add Peppers
  3. Add Scotch bonnet
  4. Add Saltfish
  5. Add ackee
  6. Add further seasoning to taste
  7. Leave for 25 minutes to simmer and marinate
  8. Serve with Coco Bread or rice
